Common questions

I lost progress / switched devices.
Progress is stored on-device by default. To sync across devices, link your account using Sign in with Apple or Google (in Settings/Account inside the app).

How do I report a bug?
Email us with your device model, iOS version, and what happened. Screenshots help a lot.

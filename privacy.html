Privacy Policy — Space Battle Legends

Last updated: 2026-02-24

Overview

Space Battle Legends uses a server backend to provide multiplayer gameplay, account functionality, and game progression. By default, the app creates a device-based player profile on first launch. You can optionally link your progress using Sign in with Apple or Google to use the same account across devices.

Data we collect

We collect the following categories of data for app functionality:

  • Identifiers: a user ID (server account ID) and a device identifier used for device-based login.
  • Account information (optional): if you use Sign in with Apple or Google, we may receive a stable account identifier. Apple may share your email address only if you choose to share it.
  • Gameplay data: deck selections, card levels, trophies, match results, progression, and related in-game state stored on our servers.
  • Messages (if used): clan chat messages and friend-to-friend messages you send through the app.
  • Product interaction: in-game actions related to gameplay and progression (for example, claiming rewards or opening chests).

In-app purchases: This version does not offer real-money in-app purchases. If we add purchases in the future, we will update this policy.

How we use data

  • To create and manage your account and allow you to play multiplayer matches.
  • To save your progression and sync it across devices when you link an account.
  • To provide social features such as clans and messaging.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse (for example, detecting suspicious behavior).

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as needed to operate the service:

  • Apple / Google: for optional sign-in authentication.
  • Service providers: hosting and infrastructure providers used to run the game servers.

Data retention

We retain account and gameplay data as long as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion at any time.

Your choices and deletion requests

You can request access to or deletion of your account data by emailing: gomerd.haman12@gmail.com. Please include your in-game name and, if possible, your user ID (shown in the app settings/about screen if available).

Children

Space Battle Legends is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
